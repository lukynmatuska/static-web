Start@FIT česky  Українська версія  Link tree Freshmen's guide

TLDR:

  • Thursday 15th September 2022, 9:30 AM, meet you at FIT
  • On Thursday, we will show you how our information system works, how to build your schedule and how to enroll in courses and register your actual classes. This training will be conducted by your fellow students. There will be two time slots in English. You have to reserve your seat in advance. There will also be a slightly different slot specifically for Erasmus students at 15:00, you don't have to make a reservation for this one.
  • On Friday, you'll have to attend the matriculation ceremony (only if you are NOT an Erasmus student of course). It's going to take place in the D105 lecture hall. You're divided into three groups based on the first letter(s) of your last name (this division is compulsory!):
    • 1. group: A–Kl: be there at 8:00
    • 2. group: Km–P: be there at 10:00
    • 3. group: R–Ž: be there at 12:00
  • On Saturday, you're going to register your classes. In the evening, you can check out some of our favourite pubs in Brno and have a chat with us at Tour de Pub.
  • On Sunday, you can come have a game of pool, table football or your favourite board game in our students' club U Kachničky or participate in our game called Brnění where you'll discover interesting places in our lovely city. We would also like to invite all our girl students to the Girls@FIT event. We know it isn't easy to get to know each other and find girl friends here at FIT so we came up with this informal meeting in form of speed friending. In the evening, you may join us for a barbecue in one of Brno's largest parks called Lužánky.
